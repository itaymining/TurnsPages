About Turns

Turns is a simple and elegant app designed to help groups and teams manage turn-taking fairly and efficiently. Whether you're organizing chores, scheduling meetings, or managing any recurring task, Turns makes it easy to see whose turn it is today, this week, or this month.

How It Works

Turns uses a deterministic algorithm based on a hash of your participant list and the current date. This ensures that:

  • The turn order is consistent and predictable
  • Everyone gets a fair rotation
  • The order remains stable across devices when shared
